# Bobcoin Vision

**Goal:** Create the "GOAT" cryptocurrency token focused on anonymity, speed, and rewarding healthy human activity.

## Core Technical Specs
*   **Base Architecture:** Hybrid of **Solana** (Speed/Volume) and **Monero** (Privacy).
*   **Privacy:** Stronger protections than Monero by default. Total anonymity.
*   **Transaction Speed:** Instant sending.
*   **Volume:** Extremely high transaction volume capability.
*   **Fees:** Low/No fees (Tip economy).

## Mining & Economy ("Proof of Dance")
*   **Mining Mechanism:** Unique process based on **Dancing** and physical exercise.
    *   *Concept:* Arcade games/exercise machines double as miners/nodes.
    *   *Goal:* Dissuade industrial mining farms ("rich get richer"). Reward healthy exercise.
*   **Network:** Arcade machines/Exercise nodes also serve as:
    *   Megatorrent nodes.
    *   Tor nodes.
    *   Distributed storage endpoints.

## Ecosystem & Use Cases
1.  **Arcade/Exercise Economy:** Tournament economy for music/rhythm games and exercise games.
2.  **Social/Dating:** Currency for dating apps, rewarding healthy adult relationships and friendships.
3.  **Gaming:** In-game economy for Retro MMORPGs.
4.  **Philosophy:** Dissuade hoarding and classism. Promote social interaction and health.

## Development Strategy
*   **Integration:** If the parent project involves tokens, Bobcoin is the standard.
*   **Compatibility:** Must be fast and anonymous.
